### Platform-Specific Adjustments

**For Midjourney v6:**
- Append `--ar 2:3 --v 6 --style raw --q 2` to each prompt

- Remove the `--ar` and parameter suffixes
- Add at the end of each prompt: "Render as a photorealistic studio product photograph, 2:3 portrait aspect ratio, white balance 5500K, no watermarks, no text overlays."
- DALL-E responds well to the detailed lighting descriptions already included

**For Flux 1.1 Pro:**
- Flux handles long-form natural language well — use prompts as written
- Add: "photorealistic, product photography, studio lighting, ultra-detailed fabric texture, 8K resolution"
- Flux particularly excels at the gradient designs (Design 1, 8, 10) and embroidery texture (Design 3, 5, 7)

**For Stable Diffusion (SDXL):**
- Break prompts into comma-separated clauses for token efficiency
- Add: `masterpiece, best quality, ultra-detailed, product photography`
- Negative prompt additions: `(deformed:1.3), (ugly:1.2), (bad anatomy:1.2), blurry, out of focus`

### Recommended Generation Order

Start with Design 3 (minimal, highest success rate on first pass), then Design 1 (gradient), then
Design 7 (lotus embroidery). These three establish the visual language of the collection. Use
successful generations from these three as style references for the more complex designs (6, 9, 10).
