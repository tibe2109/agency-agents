## CONCEPT

The FOX rendered as if carved from crystal or ice—its body composed of geometric facets that catch light and refract it in different directions. This celebrates clarity of vision, transparency in operations, and the brilliant precision of FPT's technology and people.

The gradient moves from pale ice blue (foundation) through medium sky blue (current state) to deep sapphire blue (vision fulfilled).

## CRAFTSMANSHIP DETAILS

### Embroidery Composition:

- **Fox Body**: 12cm tall, composed entirely of geometric faceted shapes
  - Head: 6-8 triangular facets creating a crystalline profile
  - Body: Overlapping parallelograms suggesting musculature
  - Legs: Vertical facet lines creating structural appearance
  - Tail: Flowing series of diamond shapes with directional stitching

- **Facet Technique**:
  - Each facet outlined in fine silver metallic thread (couching)
  - Interior of each facet filled with different shades of blue thread
  - Directional long-and-short stitch suggests light bouncing off crystal surfaces
  - Darker blue toward bottom facets, lighter toward top
  - Creates illusion of transparency and internal light

- **Light Refraction Effect**:
  - Small highlights in white or pale gold thread at facet angles
  - Creates three-dimensional depth
  - Suggests actual light refraction through crystal

- **Background Embellishment** (optional):
  - 4-6 small snowflake or star-burst shapes around fox
  - Emphasizes crystalline theme
  - Stitched in silver metallic thread
  - Minimal and delicate

### Silk Specifications:
- Habotai weave (smooth surface for light reflection)
- Hand-dyed gradient: pale to deep blue
- Light sizing only (preserve drape)
- Metallic thread sheen enhanced by fabric finish

## NARRATIVE

### The Crystal Metaphor:

In Vietnamese culture, crystal and jade represent transparency, purity, and the clarity of thought. A crystalline fox symbolizes not just beauty but also precision, honesty, and the ability to see through to what matters most.

**For Leadership:**
Top 50 FPT employees are the "crystal clear" thinkers—they see problems transparently and craft brilliant solutions. Wearing this sash is a reminder of the clarity and precision that defines their work.

**The Geometry Connection:**
For a tech company, geometric/faceted design speaks directly to algorithmic thinking and structural precision. The crystalline fox celebrates the intersection of beauty and engineering.

**Light Refraction = Possibility:**
Crystal doesn't just reflect light; it refracts it—breaking white light into infinite colors. Similarly, FPT's top people take simple inputs and transform them into multi-colored possibilities. They create value through precision.
