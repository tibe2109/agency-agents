## CONCEPT

A DNA helix rising vertically up the sash, with one strand in blue (representing heritage/DNA of Vietnamese business) and one strand in orange/tech colors (representing technology). Key years and milestones marked at intervals (1997, 2007, 2017, 2027), showing FPT's biological-like growth—not just business expansion but evolutionary development.

## CRAFTSMANSHIP DETAILS

### Embroidery:

- **DNA Helix Structure**: Full vertical helix, approximately 18cm tall
  - Blue strand on left: Represents heritage and foundational DNA
  - Orange strand on right: Represents technology and innovation
  - Connecting links between strands: Silver metallic thread, perpendicular to main strands
  - Approximately 8-10 complete "turns" of the helix

- **Milestone Markers**:
  - "1997" at base in fine serif font (gold embroidery)
  - "2007" at lower-middle (1/3 point)
  - "2017" at middle (1/2 point)
  - "2027" at top (goal point)
  - Optional: Small icons at each milestone (tech symbols, business symbols)

- **Technique**:
  - Backstitch for helix outline
  - Long and short stitch for strand shading (darker at base, lighter at top for optimism)
  - Couching for connecting links
  - Fine satin stitch for year labels

## NARRATIVE

DNA represents not just life but the very code that defines an organism. By visualizing FPT's heritage as a helix—with one strand being pure business DNA and one strand being pure technology DNA—we celebrate how these two elements have intertwined and evolved together over 30 years.

The timeline shows not just growth but purposeful evolution, each decade building on the previous one, creating an unbroken chain of achievement.
