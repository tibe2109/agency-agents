## CONCEPT

The FOX mascot with delicate angel-like or phoenix-like wings, soaring in flight against a three-zone gradient sky. This design celebrates innovation and the transformative power of FPT's technology — suggesting that the company and its top employees have achieved something transcendent.

Visual metaphor: FPT's spirit takes flight into the future, unbound by earthly constraints.

## CRAFTSMANSHIP DETAILS

### Embroidery Composition:

- **Fox Body**: 10cm tall, centered, rendered in split and satin stitch
  - Head turned slightly upward as if gazing at sky
  - Body in graceful S-curve suggesting flight motion
  - Tail flowing and dynamic, not grounded

- **Wings**: Appliqué technique using ivory or pale pearl silk
  - Two large wings, one on each side of fox body
  - Dimensions: 8cm wingspan each
  - Outline embroidered in fine gold metallic thread (couching technique)
  - Internal feather details stitched with white and pale grey thread
  - Layered appearance creates dimensional illusion

- **Flight Path Suggestion**:
  - Curved metallic lines below fox suggesting upward motion
  - Approximately 4-5 curved lines, decreasing length
  - Creates sense of momentum and ascension

- **Sky Elements**:
  - Small embroidered stars or light-burst shapes
  - Cloud-like shapes rendered in pale thread
  - Optional: constellation patterns for tech reference

### Silk Specifications:
- Jacquard base with subtle texture
- Three-zone gradient dye: deep ocean at hem, turquoise in middle, gold at shoulder
- Appliqué silk in contrasting cream or ivory tone
- Soft finishing to preserve flowing drape

## STAGE PRESENCE

**Visual Impact:** From stage, this sash creates the illusion of movement. The upward gradient combined with the flying fox creates a "reaching toward excellence" visual narrative that's immediately understood by the awards audience.

**Lighting Dynamics:**
- Warm uplighting: Gold zone shines, wings catch specular highlights
- Cool sidelighting: Creates shadow detail in wing feather structure
- Animated effect: As wearer moves, wings appear to flutter slightly

**Distance Recognition:**
- 20m: "Colorful sash with gradient and flying creature"
- 5m: "Wings! This fox is flying!"
- In hand: Every feather detail visible, appliqué technique appreciated

**Ceremonial Impact:**
Best for innovation awards, technology leaders, and visionary employees. The transcendence theme appeals to those who've driven FPT's digital transformation.

## NARRATIVE

### The Transcendence Theme:

In Vietnamese tradition, the ability to transform (human to spirit, mortal to immortal) is seen as the ultimate achievement. By giving the FOX wings, this design celebrates FPT's employees as transformative agents—people who've elevated themselves and the company beyond ordinary business.

**For the Top 50:**
These employees are not just workers; they are innovators whose contributions have allowed FPT to "take flight." The wings symbolize their freedom to create, imagine, and pursue excellence without earthly constraints.

**Technology Integration:**
Subtle constellation patterns or geometric shapes in the sky reference the digital world while maintaining elegance. This bridges the gap between "high tech" and "high fashion."

**The Turquoise Bridge:**
The cyan/turquoise middle zone is deliberately chosen to represent the digital/tech space—it's the color of innovation, AI, and digital transformation. The fox flying through it suggests mastery over technology itself.
