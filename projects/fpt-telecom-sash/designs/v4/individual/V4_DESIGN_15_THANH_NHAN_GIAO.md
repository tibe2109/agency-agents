## CONCEPT

Five interlocking shapes (hearts or circular forms) representing different FPT communities (employees, customers, partners, society, innovation). Rendered in gradient reds and creams, with gold metallic connecting lines. Emphasizes that FPT's true success is measured in human connections and community impact.
