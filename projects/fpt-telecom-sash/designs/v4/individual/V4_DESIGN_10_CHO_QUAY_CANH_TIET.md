## CONCEPT

The FOX positioned in a serene bamboo forest, surrounded by stylized bamboo stalks and leaves. The fox appears calm and contemplative, in harmony with nature. This celebrates FPT's commitment to sustainability, growth, and the balance between technology and natural wisdom.

Bamboo in Vietnamese culture represents strength, resilience, and rapid growth—all core FPT values.

## CRAFTSMANSHIP DETAILS

### Embroidery Composition:

- **Fox**: 11cm tall, rendered in warm browns and greens for integration into environment
  - Sitting or standing peacefully
  - Expression serene, almost meditating
  - Subtle integration of fox colors with surrounding forest

- **Bamboo Forest**:
  - 8-12 bamboo stalks of varying heights (3-15cm)
  - Each stalk rendered in warm brown thread with dark outlines
  - Small branches extending from stalks in various green shades
  - Leaf details stitched in multiple shades of green using small split stitches

- **Depth Creation**:
  - Stalks in foreground: darker, more detailed
  - Stalks in background: lighter, less defined (atmospheric perspective)
  - Creates illusion of forest depth

- **Optional Ground Element**:
  - Small moss or grass details around fox's feet
  - Suggests sitting in natural clearing

## NARRATIVE

Bamboo is more than decoration—it's a symbol of Vietnamese resilience and virtue. The fox in the bamboo forest represents FPT's employees as rooted in nature and sustainability while maintaining the grace and intelligence of the fox—the bridge between wilderness and civilization.

This design appeals to environmentally conscious leaders and those who value harmony with nature alongside technological innovation.
