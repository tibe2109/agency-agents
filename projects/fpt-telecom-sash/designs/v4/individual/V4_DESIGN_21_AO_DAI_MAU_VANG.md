## CONCEPT

A stylized áo dài silhouette (Vietnam's traditional graceful dress) rendered in intricate gold embroidery on a golden gradient background. The design celebrates Vietnamese femininity, elegance, and cultural pride. This design speaks to FPT's deep roots in Vietnamese culture and its commitment to making Vietnam proud on the global stage.

The áo dài's flowing panels and elegant proportions represent the balance between tradition and modernity that FPT embodies.

## NARRATIVE

The áo dài is Vietnam's most recognizable symbol of national identity. By rendering it in gold and positioning it on a FPT sash, we make a bold statement: FPT employees are ambassadors of Vietnamese culture and excellence. They represent Vietnam's grace, tradition, and modern achievement simultaneously.
