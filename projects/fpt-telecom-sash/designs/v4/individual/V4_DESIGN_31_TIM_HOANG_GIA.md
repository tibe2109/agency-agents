## CONCEPT

A single, pure purple color rendered in a seamless vertical gradient from deep indigo (#4b0082) at the base through rich royal purple (#9370db) to pale lavender (#e6d7f5) at the top. No embroidery. No pattern. No decoration. The sash IS the color. This represents the ultimate luxury principle: that true sophistication needs no decoration—the material and gradient speak entirely.

In traditional luxury (Hermès, Bottega Veneta), the most expensive pieces are often the simplest. This design celebrates that philosophy.

## NARRATIVE

This design is for the leader who doesn't need decoration. The executive who understands that power and sophistication speak through subtlety. A person wearing V4_31 makes a bold anti-statement: "I am secure enough that I don't need embroidery or pattern to prove my status."

This sash costs the same as V4_01 (with embroidery) but in some ways is MORE valuable because it requires perfect dye work. A single imperfection in the gradient is visible. There is nowhere to hide. This is transparency and purity.

It's the sash equivalent of a white shirt from Hermès—simple, expensive, and speaks volumes about the wearer's refined taste.
