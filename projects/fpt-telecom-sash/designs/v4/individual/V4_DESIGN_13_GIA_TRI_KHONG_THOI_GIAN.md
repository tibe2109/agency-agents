## CONCEPT

A horizontal timeline bar running across each panel, with significant milestone years marked above and below. Color transitions from dark (foundation) through cyan (growth) to brilliant gold (achievement). Each milestone gets a small embroidered marker or icon representing that period's contribution.

This design literally shows the 30-year journey visually—perfect for ceremonies celebrating longevity.

## CRAFTSMANSHIP DETAILS

### Timeline Structure:

- **Main Bar**: Horizontal line running ~2cm from the sash center
  - Width: Full panel width (approximately 12-14cm visible)
  - Height/Thickness: 0.8cm gradient filled
  - Bottom edge: Navy, gradually transitions to cyan in middle, gold at right end
  - Creates visual "rising" effect as timeline progresses

- **Year Markers**: Above and below the main bar at each significant year
  - Years marked: 1997, 2002, 2007, 2012, 2017, 2022, 2027
  - Each year in fine serif font (0.3cm height)
  - Alternating above/below bar for visual interest

- **Milestone Icons**:
  - 1997: Starting point marker (simple circle)
  - 2002: Business milestone icon
  - 2007: Market expansion icon
  - 2012: Technology advancement icon
  - 2017: National achievement icon
  - 2022: Digital transformation icon
  - 2027: Future vision icon (starburst or beacon)

- **Texture Detail**: Small embroidered texture within the bar suggesting accumulated value
