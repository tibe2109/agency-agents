## CONCEPT

The FOX mascot (Chó Quay = playful term of affection in Vietnamese) in a joyful prancing pose, celebrating the 30th anniversary. This is the "cute but dignified" version — the fox's expression is proud and noble, not cartoonish. The golden color palette represents success, prosperity, and the warmth of FPT's community.

**Visual Story:**
> A confident FOX mid-leap, one front paw raised, tail flowing in graceful arc. The fox's eyes show wisdom and joy simultaneously. Around it, subtle embroidered rays of light suggesting movement and energy. The overall impression: "This is a mascot who has earned its place of honor."

## CRAFTSMANSHIP DETAILS

### Embroidery Composition:
- **FOX Figure**: Long and short stitch technique, 15cm height, centered on each panel
  - Head: Fine split stitch for ear definition, round eyes in black French knots
  - Body: Satin stitch with directional shading (left-to-right gradient)
  - Tail: Couching technique with metallic gold outline, flowing motion
  - Paws: Raised satin stitch for 3D effect, clear pad definition

- **Rays Around Fox**: 12 radiating lines of 22K gold metallic thread
  - Straight lines emanating from fox's position
  - Spacing: 30mm apart
  - Length: 4-8cm depending on position

- **Bottom Corner Badge**: FPT Telecom wordmark in 0.5cm sans-serif capitals
  - Technique: Satin stitch lettering
  - Thread: 22K gold metallic
  - Placement: 3cm from hem, 2cm from side

### Silk Specifications:
- Base weave: Jacquard with subtle texture
- Hand-painted dyeing effect on cream base
- Golden wash to create luminosity

## STAGE PRESENCE

**Visual Impact:** When worn on stage during the FPT 30th Anniversary Awards ceremony, the golden fox creates an immediate focal point. The warm golden tone stands out beautifully against navy or dark formal wear.

**Lighting Performance:**
- Warm yellow uplighting (2700K): Makes golden fox radiate warmth and celebration
- Cool spotlighting (5600K): Fox maintains integrity, cream background prevents washed-out appearance
- Raking light from side: Embroidery raises away from background, creating visible relief

**Distance Reading:**
- 20m away: Instantly recognizable as "FOX on golden background"
- 5m away: Expressive face visible, rays of light create sense of movement
- In hand: Rich embroidery detail, every stitch tells the "prancing" story

**Ceremonial Notes:**
> Perfect for mid-tier awards recipients — conveys joy and recognition without being overly formal. Works exceptionally well on-camera for photo ops.

## VISUAL DIRECTION

### Studio Setup:
- **Key Light**: 120cm octabox overhead at 45-degree angle
- **Fill**: Bounce card from camera right at 2:1 ratio
- **Backdrop**: Neutral warm grey (#D4D4D4)
- **Lens**: 90mm macro, f/8 aperture
- **Position**: 3/4 view showing full fox figure on one panel

### Styling Notes:
- Lay sash flat on seamless paper
- Show natural drape of silk
- Ensure metallic gold embroidery catches light
- Highlight textural differences between satin stitch and surrounding silk

## NARRATIVE

### Why This Design for TOP50FTEL & FTEL30:

The golden prancing fox represents the spirit of FPT's top 50 employees: energetic, forward-moving, successful, and deserving of celebration. Unlike the formal ocean gradient of Design 01, this design radiates JOY and ACHIEVEMENT.

**The Emoji Factor:** FOX (Chó Quay) is FPT's beloved mascot. When employees see this design, they immediately feel the personal connection to the brand. The prancing pose isn't just cute — it's the visual equivalent of a standing ovation. The fox isn't sitting; it's *celebrating them*.

**Color Psychology for Awards:**
- Golden yellow = Success, prosperity, warmth
- Cream background = Luxury, exclusivity (not everyone gets the golden fox)
- Metallic rays = Radiant energy, "you are the light of FPT"

**The "Cute but Dignified" Balance:**
The fox's expression and posture prevent it from feeling cartoonish. The embroidery technique (long and short stitch with realistic proportions) makes the fox look like a noble creature, not a toy. The radiating gold rays elevate the entire composition to ceremonial importance.

**Wearability:**
Employees can wear this sash to formal events, award ceremonies, or company celebrations. The joy radiates outward, making the wearer feel celebrated and elevated.
