## CONCEPT

Three stylized Vietnamese characters (Phúc/福 - Happiness, Lộc/祿 - Prosperity, Thọ/壽 - Longevity) rendered in large, elegant calligraphy style embroidery with gold metallic accents. These three "fortunes" are the highest blessings in Vietnamese culture. Wearing this sash is to wish oneself and others these three blessings.
