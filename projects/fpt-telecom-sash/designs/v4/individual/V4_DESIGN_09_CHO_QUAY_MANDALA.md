## CONCEPT

The FOX positioned at the center of a geometric mandala pattern—nested concentric circles and polygons radiating outward, each zone representing a year or phase of FPT's 30-year journey. The fox is not separate from the mandala but its sacred heart.

Symbolism: FPT's success is not random but ordered by universal principles of harmony and balance.

## CRAFTSMANSHIP DETAILS

### Embroidery Composition:

- **Central Fox**: 8cm tall, rendered in fine satin stitch at dead center
- **Ring Pattern 1** (around fox): 8-12 small diamond shapes, 2cm each
- **Ring Pattern 2**: Circular chain of triangles, 3.5cm from center
- **Ring Pattern 3**: Larger geometric polygons (hexagons, octagons), 5cm from center
- **Ring Pattern 4**: Outermost pattern—radiating lines suggesting expansion, reaching toward edges

**Total Coverage**: Approximately 60% of panel filled with embroidery

**Technique**:
- Center fox in navy with gold highlights
- Each ring transitions color from inner blue through bronze to outer bright gold
- Directional stitching creates visual movement outward
- Metallic thread couching on ring outlines for definition

## NARRATIVE

The mandala represents cosmic order—the belief that everything in the universe follows mathematical harmony. By positioning the FOX at the center of a mandala, we celebrate FPT's role in bringing order, harmony, and purposeful innovation to the Vietnamese tech ecosystem.

For top employees, this says: "You are not just workers; you are custodians of harmony and balance that radiates outward to affect the entire industry."
