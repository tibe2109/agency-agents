## CONCEPT

The FOX surrounded by or emerging from stylized flames, suggesting passionate energy, determination, and the transformative power of FPT's innovation. The flames are not destructive but illuminating—they represent the burning desire to achieve excellence that characterizes FPT's top 50 employees.

Color flow mimics natural flame: deep burgundy (coals) → orange (flame) → gold (luminosity).

## CRAFTSMANSHIP DETAILS

### Embroidery Composition:

- **Fox Figure**: 11cm tall, centered on each panel
  - Rendered in split and satin stitch
  - Expression shows alertness and fierce pride
  - Body positioned as if running or leaping through flames
  - Outlined in copper metallic thread for warmth

- **Flame Elements**:
  - 8-12 stylized flame shapes surrounding the fox
  - Each flame 4-10cm tall
  - Executed in gradient embroidery technique (darker at base, lighter at tip)
  - Base of flames: burgundy/deep red thread
  - Mid-flame: orange and gold thread blend
  - Flame tips: bright gold metallic couching

- **Technique**:
  - Long and short stitch for smooth flame color transitions
  - Directional stitching to suggest upward movement
  - Metallic couching on top for shimmer effect
  - Creates illusion of flickering light

- **Central Glow**:
  - Area around fox rendered lighter
  - Suggests fox is source of light, not consumed by fire
  - Reinforces metaphor: "Fox is the illuminating force"

### Silk Specifications:
- Jacquard base with subtle texture
- Hand-painted gradient dye (burgundy to orange to gold)
- Metallic finish on selected areas to catch light
- Soft hand to preserve drape

## STAGE PRESENCE

**Visual Impact:** Dramatic and commanding. The warm color palette reads powerfully on stage, suggesting controlled passion and focused energy. Even from 20m away, the sash radiates intensity.

**Lighting Response:**
- Warm uplighting (2700K): Flames appear to glow from within
- Cool downlighting: Burgundy deepens, creates shadow drama
- Moving light source: Creates illusion of actual flame flicker

**Distance Reading:**
- 20m: "Dramatic warm-colored sash with dynamic imagery"
- 5m: "Fox surrounded by flames—what intensity!"
- In hand: Every flame layer visible, metallic details catch light

**Ceremonial Context:**
Perfect for high-achievement awards, innovation leaders, or "Most Passionate Employee" recognition. The flame imagery conveys the burning energy these leaders bring to FPT.

## NARRATIVE

### The Fire Metaphor in Asian Culture:

Fire represents transformation, passion, and divine illumination in Vietnamese and Asian cultures. Unlike Western traditions where fire often symbolizes destruction, Eastern philosophy sees fire as purifying and enlightening.

**For Top Performers:**
These employees "burn bright" with passion for their work. The fox surrounded by flames doesn't fear the fire—it is the fire. This celebrates individuals whose energy and commitment literally drive FPT's transformation.

**The Glow Effect:**
By positioning the fox as the light source (not the victim of flames), we celebrate how these leaders illuminate the path forward for the entire organization. They don't just work harder; they inspire others through their intensity.

**Wearability:**
Employees can wear this sash to motivate themselves and others. The visual reminder: "Remember the passion that drives us forward."
