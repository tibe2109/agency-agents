## CONCEPT

A single fox profile rendered through reverse appliqué (cutting away background to reveal underlying gold leaf or metallic fabric), combined with traditional gold foil embossing. The fox appears to be made of pure gold—capturing the prestige and honor of FPT's top 50 employees.

## CRAFTSMANSHIP DETAILS

### Technique:
- **Reverse Appliqué**: Top layer of cream/pale gold silk carefully cut away to reveal
- **Underlying Material**: Actual 22-karat gold leaf or gold-foil backed fabric beneath the cutaway
- **Fox Shape**: 13cm tall, rendered as a perfect silhouette profile
- **Edge Binding**: Very fine couching in gold metallic thread to prevent fraying and create crisp edges
- **Gold Foil Embossing**: Additional tooled gold foil work on the fox's body for dimensional relief

### Execution Requirements:
- Requires master craftsperson (museum-quality work)
- Each piece is semi-unique due to hand-finishing
- Gold foil application must be precise to avoid flaking
- Testing and quality control essential

## NARRATIVE

In Vietnamese culture, gold represents ultimate honor, success, and the sacred. By rendering the fox entirely in gold—not as embroidery but as actual gold leaf—this sash elevates the wearer to the status of a sacred figure, a guardian of FPT's most precious values.

This is the most expensive design to produce but also the most memorable. Employees wearing this sash will feel the literal weight of honor around their shoulders.
