# DESIGN V4_22 — "NÓN LÁ HIỆN ĐẠI"
## Conical Hat Modern - Tradition Meets Technology

The iconic Vietnamese conical hat (nón lá) rendered with modern geometric overlays and digital connectivity elements. The hat itself is rendered in pale green (bamboo color), while geometric lines and nodes suggest technological networks. Represents the fusion of Vietnamese tradition with digital modernity.
