## CONCEPT

A pure silhouette of the FOX in a dynamic leaping or running pose, rendered as a completely filled shape against a subtle gradient background. The fox itself is filled with tonal gradations creating depth without internal detail—just like a beautiful shadow cast by sunset light.

This celebrates motion, urgency, and the dynamic forward momentum of FPT.

## CRAFTSMANSHIP DETAILS

### Embroidery:
- **Fox Silhouette**: 14cm tall, completely filled with thread
- **Pose**: Dynamic leap or bound, legs extended, suggesting motion
- **Filling Technique**: Long and short stitch creating smooth tonal gradient within the silhouette
  - Darkest grey at bottom (grounded)
  - Lighter grey toward center (movement)
  - Pale grey/white near upper edges (light hitting)
- **Outline**: Fine backstitch in charcoal, perfectly sharp edges

### Execution:
- No internal detail lines (eyes, etc.)—pure silhouette
- Gradient within the filled shape creates dimension
- Background remains unembroidered, allowing silk sheen to show
- Approximately 65-70% of panel is embroidered fox shape

## NARRATIVE

Silhouette art represents the moment when we see someone in shadow—when their actions speak louder than details. This design celebrates the essence of these leaders: their motion, their drive, their forward momentum matter more than decoration or explanation.
