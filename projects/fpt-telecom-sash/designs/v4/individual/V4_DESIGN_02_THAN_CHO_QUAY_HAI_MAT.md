## CONCEPT

Two fox profiles facing opposite directions, sharing a single eye in the center. One fox looks to the past (darker, serious expression), one to the future (lighter, hopeful expression). The background gradient shifts from dark slate through royal purple to bright gold — representing the journey from 1997 (foundation) to 2027 (vision forward).

This is the "wise ancestor spirit" interpretation of the FOX mascot — a creature that honors heritage while boldly pursuing the future.

## CRAFTSMANSHIP DETAILS

### Embroidery Composition:

- **Dual Fox Profiles**: Each 12cm tall, facing opposite directions
  - Left fox (Past): Rendered in darker metallics (oxidized silver, bronze)
  - Right fox (Future): Rendered in bright gold metallics
  - Shared Central Eye: Large 2cm diameter circle in contrasting color

- **Technique**: Split stitch for outlines, long and short stitch for shading
  - Past fox shows closed or contemplative expression
  - Future fox shows open, alert, forward-looking expression
  - Eye-sharing creates visual unity

- **Connecting Elements**:
  - Concentric circles around the central eye
  - Radiating lines suggesting energy and continuity
  - Total 8-10 rings expanding outward

- **Text Integration**:
  - Bottom left: "1997" in small serif font
  - Bottom right: "2027" in same serif font
  - Center bottom: "FPT Telecom" wordmark

### Silk Specifications:
- Habotai weave with visible texture
- Hand-painted gradient (slate to purple to gold)
- No banding — smooth color transition
- Light fabric finishing to preserve natural drape

## STAGE PRESENCE

**Visual Impact:** The dual-face design creates conversation. From 20m away, viewers see a striking purple-to-gold gradient with fox silhouettes. Up close, the symbolic meaning becomes clear: honoring past while building future.

**Lighting Scenarios:**
- Warm uplighting (2700K): Emphasizes gold zone, creates warmth
- Cool downlighting (5600K): Highlights purple mystery, adds depth
- Raking light: Embroidery relief becomes visible, creating 3D illusion

**Distance Reading:**
- 20m: "Gradient sash with symmetrical fox pattern"
- 5m: "Two foxes, one eye — what's the meaning?"
- In hand: Full realization — "This honors past & future"

**Ceremonial Context:**
Perfect for long-service award recipients and company leadership. The "looking both ways" metaphor resonates with those who've helped build FPT and now lead the vision forward.

---

## VISUAL DIRECTION

### Studio Setup:
- Position sash diagonally to emphasize gradient sweep
- Warm-cool lighting split: warm from left (past/gold), cool from right (future/slate)
- Soft key light from overhead at 60 degrees
- Fill card positioned to reduce harsh shadows on dark areas

### Composition:
- Show complete gradient journey across both panels
- Embroidery must catch light to show relief and metallic details
- Emphasize the central eye as the focal point

## NARRATIVE

### Why "Thần Chó Quay Hai Mặt" Resonates:

In Vietnamese mythology, the concept of duality (past-future, yin-yang, old-young) represents spiritual wisdom. By giving the FOX two faces—one reflecting on achievement, one envisioning the future—this design celebrates the top 50 FPT employees as custodians of a living legacy.

**For Award Recipients:**
- Leaders feel honored as architects of both past success and future vision
- Creates a powerful symbol: "Your work honors those before you and inspires those after"
- The shared eye suggests clear vision and continuity of purpose

**The Purple Choice:**
Purple traditionally represents transformation and nobility in Asian cultures. Here it bridges past (slate) and future (gold), suggesting that FPT's transformation over 30 years is noble, purposeful, and ongoing.

**Luxury & Meaning:**
Unlike purely decorative designs, this sash invites contemplation. Wearers carry a philosophical statement: honoring heritage while building the future. This appeals to FPT's intellectual and visionary leadership.
