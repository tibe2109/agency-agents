## CRAFTSMANSHIP DETAILS

### Embroidery Composition:

- **Lotus Flowers**: 3 major blooms, graduated sizes (8cm, 6cm, 4cm diameter)
  - Each bloom composed of 20-30 individual petals
  - Petals arranged in naturalistic overlapping pattern (not symmetrical)
  - Each petal executed with long and short stitch for dimensional shading
  - Petals raised away from background using padding technique (multiple layers of thread underneath)
  - Petal edges carefully blended for organic appearance
  - Central stigma (flower center) in bright gold thread with fine detail work

- **Stems and Leaves**:
  - Green thread in split stitch following natural curves
  - Leaf detail rendered with fine vein work
  - Stems appear to support the blooms structurally

- **Water/Background**:
  - Subtle wave pattern in pale thread representing water from which lotus rises
  - Background serves to frame rather than compete with flowers

- **Total Embroidery**: Approximately 35-40% of panel surface
- **Relief Height**: Petals raised 5-8mm above background silk

### Production Requirements:
- Master embroiderer with specialized training
- Requires linen thread backing for support
- Careful tension control to prevent puckering
- Quality assurance for each petal detail
- Estimated 40-60 hours per sash
