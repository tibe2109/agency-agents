## TROUBLESHOOTING GEMINI PRO GENERATION

**Problem**: Image doesn't look like the design description
**Solution**: Try a different variant or adjust lighting description in prompt

**Problem**: Colors are wrong (too saturated, wrong hue)
**Solution**: This is normal for Gemini—select variant closest to hex codes, supplier will color-correct in production

**Problem**: Embroidery looks flat or undetailed
**Solution**: Regenerate with "macro lens, raking light, complete sharpness" emphasis in prompt

**Problem**: Fabric looks synthetic or plasticky
**Solution**: Gemini sometimes renders silk as satin—supplier knows to use actual mulberry silk (texture will be correct in production)

**Problem**: Can't get the gradient right (banding visible)
**Solution**: Request "seamless ombré" and "zero banding" in regeneration—Gemini will improve
