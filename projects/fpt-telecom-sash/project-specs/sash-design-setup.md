# 🎗️ FPT Telecom Top 50 Excellence Sash Design Project

## 📋 Project Overview

**Objective**: Design a prestigious honor sash (khăn choàng cổ vinh danh) that celebrates top 50 outstanding employees of FPT Telecom during the 30th anniversary celebration.

**Significance**: This sash is not just an award—it's a **symbol of recognition, pride, and legacy** that will commemorate 30 years of pioneering spirit and relentless progress.

---

## 🎯 Core Theme & Narrative

### Main Slogan
**"Khí phách tiên phong"** (Pioneering Spirit / Bold Trailblazer Spirit)

### Narrative Arc
- **Journey**: 30 years of FPT Telecom's founding and growth
- **Recipients**: Loyal, resilient employees who have contributed to breakthrough achievements
- **Emotion**: Pride, recognition, belonging to a legacy
- **Vision**: This sash is a **100-year heirloom**—something recipients will treasure forever
- **Story**: Collaborative creation by leadership, HR departments, top employees, and world-class designers

---

## 📦 Deliverables

### Design Deliverables
1. **Color Palette** (3-5 final options with psychology justification)
2. **Material & Texture** (fabric specification, quality grade)
3. **Layout & Composition** (main design, emblem placement, typography)
4. **Typography & Messaging** (slogan, 30-year mark, recipient name area)
5. **Emblem/Logo Integration** (FPT Telecom branding, 30-year badge)
6. **Visual Mockups** (2D and 3D renders of final design)
7. **Design Specification Document** (manufacturing guidelines, color codes, dimensions)

### Strategic Deliverables
1. **Brand Narrative Document** (how the sash tells the company story)
2. **Psychological Impact Analysis** (how colors & symbols evoke emotion)
3. **Stage Design Integration** (how the sash fits into the awards ceremony aesthetic)
4. **Wearer Experience Documentation** (how it makes recipients feel)

## 🎬 Design Pipeline Phases

### Phase 1: **Deep Discovery & Interviewing** (2-3 hours)
- Orchestrator spawns interview agents
- Conduct 20-30 min interviews with each stakeholder role
- Capture emotions, insights, symbolic preferences
- Document color/material preferences

### Phase 2: **Collective Brainstorming** (2 hours)
- Master Design Architect + All Specialists
- Synthesize interview findings
- Generate 3-5 distinct design concepts
- Each concept backed by psychological/cultural justification

### Phase 3: **Design Refinement** (2 hours)
- Color Psychology Specialist validates color choices
- Fabric Specialist recommends materials
- Stage Design Consultant ensures visual impact
- Narrative Strategist confirms story alignment

### Phase 4: **Visual Creation** (1 hour)
- Image Prompt Engineer creates mockups
- 2D design specifications
- 3D renders showing wear experience
- Stage ceremony visualization

### Phase 5: **Final Validation & Optimization** (1 hour)
- All specialists review final design
- Psychological impact assessment
- Manufacturing feasibility check
- Documentation package complete

## 💡 Design Direction Guidance

### Visual Tone
- **Not generic**: Avoid standard corporate award look
- **Heritage-focused**: Reflect 30-year journey, not just current brand
- **Emotional**: Should move people, not just inform
- **Timeless**: Must feel relevant 30+ years from now
- **Premium**: Every element screams quality and recognition

### Color Considerations (To Explore)
- **Option A**: Deep blue + gold (traditional prestige)
- **Option B**: Dynamic gradient (representing growth/journey)
- **Option C**: Company signature colors + accent (brand consistency)
- **Option D**: Earth tones + metallic (heritage, timelessness)
- *(Final colors decided after psychological analysis)*

### Symbolism to Consider
- "30" as visual element
- Pioneering spirit icon
- FPT Telecom heritage symbol
- Growth/ascension visual metaphor
- Unity/team symbolism
