# 🎤 Phase 1: Deep Discovery Interview Guides

> **Purpose**: Understand the emotional, psychological, and strategic dimensions of what this 30-year legacy sash should represent and how it should make people feel.

---

## Interview 1.1: Executive Leadership
**Duration**: 60 minutes
**Participants**: C-Suite / Strategic Leadership
**Key Goals**: Vision, values, emotional tone, brand identity, legacy message

### Section A: 30-Year Legacy & Vision (10 mins)

1. **What's the most pivotal moment in FPT Telecom's 30-year journey that shaped who we are today?**
   - *Why*: Understand what milestone should be honored
   - *Listen for*: Turning points, breakthroughs, defining moments

2. **If you had to capture the essence of FPT Telecom's evolution in a single symbol or visual, what would it be?**
   - *Why*: Get symbolic direction for design
   - *Listen for*: Imagery, metaphors, visual references

3. **How do you want employees to remember this 30-year chapter 20 years from now?**
   - *Why*: Legacy mindset
   - *Listen for*: Emotional legacy, cultural impact

4. **What does "Khí phách tiên phong" (Pioneering Spirit) mean to you personally?**
   - *Why*: Deep understanding of the slogan
   - *Listen for*: Definition of pioneering, boldness, leadership

---

### Section B: Excellence & Values (12 mins)

5. **What does excellence truly mean at FPT Telecom beyond performance metrics?**
   - *Why*: Define what the top 50 represent
   - *Listen for*: Character, resilience, values, impact

6. **The top 50 are being honored - what specific qualities or behaviors are you recognizing?**
   - *Why*: Understand what to honor in design
   - *Listen for*: Loyalty, innovation, breakthrough thinking, culture-building

7. **How do you want people to feel when they see someone wearing this sash on stage?**
   - *Why*: Emotional impact on audience
   - *Listen for*: Respect, aspiration, pride, belonging

8. **What's the difference between a "nice award" and a "legacy treasure"?**
   - *Why*: Quality & significance distinction
   - *Listen for*: Timelessness, craftsmanship, meaning depth

---

### Section C: Emotional & Psychological Impact (10 mins)

9. **When you imagine the top 50 receiving this sash, what emotion should they feel?**
   - *Why*: Primary emotional goal
   - *Listen for*: Pride, validation, belonging, elevation, legacy feeling

10. **Should this award inspire others to strive for excellence, or validate what's already there?**
    - *Why*: Award's psychological role
    - *Listen for*: Aspirational vs. recognition focus

11. **What would break your heart about how this sash is designed or presented?**
    - *Why*: Understand non-negotiables and values
    - *Listen for*: Authenticity, quality, respect concerns

12. **What would make you absolutely proud to present this to the world?**
    - *Why*: Vision of success
    - *Listen for*: Quality indicators, representation of brand

---

### Section D: Design & Symbolism (10 mins)

13. **What colors instinctively represent FPT Telecom's identity to you?**
    - *Why*: Color preferences
    - *Listen for*: Company signature colors, emotional color associations

14. **Are there specific historical moments, achievements, or company milestones that should be referenced in the design?**
    - *Why*: Historical context
    - *Listen for*: Specific symbols, dates, achievements

15. **Should the sash make recipients feel they're part of an exclusive elite, or part of a larger legacy team?**
    - *Why*: Social positioning
    - *Listen for*: Exclusivity vs. inclusion mindset

16. **If the sash "spoke," what would it say about the wearer?**
    - *Why*: Brand message conveyed
    - *Listen for*: Character, values, achievement level

---

### Section E: Quality & Material (6 mins)

17. **This sash will be worn at important personal moments - what quality level must it meet?**
    - *Why*: Material/craftsmanship standards
    - *Listen for*: Luxury, durability, premium indicators

18. **Should it feel luxurious and precious, or professional and authentic?**
    - *Why*: Tone of quality
    - *Listen for*: Balance between prestige and genuineness

---

### Section F: Stage & Ceremony (6 mins)

19. **Imagine the awards presentation - how should recipients look on stage wearing this sash?**
    - *Why*: Visual impact in ceremony
    - *Listen for*: Elegance, presence, authority, humility balance

20. **Will media and photography be present? How important is the sash's visual appearance in photos?**
    - *Why*: Media/PR dimension
    - *Listen for*: Photo-ready, timeless appearance, brand visibility

---

### Section G: Final Reflection (6 mins)

21. **If you were to write the story of why this sash matters, what would you say in 2-3 sentences?**
    - *Why*: Distill the narrative
    - *Listen for*: Core message, emotional arc, legacy statement

## Interview 1.5: Stage Design Team / Event Coordinator
**Duration**: 45 minutes
**Participants**: Ceremony director, stage designer, event coordinator, lighting designer
**Key Goals**: Stage aesthetic, lighting conditions, visual hierarchy, integration

### Section A: Ceremony Aesthetic (10 mins)

1. **What's the overall aesthetic or energy of the 30-year anniversary ceremony?**
   - *Why*: Design context
   - *Listen for*: Tone (celebratory, traditional, modern, elegant, etc.)

2. **What's the color scheme or visual theme of the stage and ceremony space?**
   - *Why*: Design integration
   - *Listen for*: Backdrop colors, lighting colors, overall palette

3. **What emotions should the ceremony evoke from start to finish?**
   - *Why*: Emotional arc
   - *Listen for*: Narrative emotions, climactic moments

---

### Section B: Sash in the Ceremony (12 mins)

4. **Where and how will the sash presentation happen? (Describe the moment)**
   - *Why*: Context for design
   - *Listen for*: Stage position, lighting, background, timing

5. **How will the recipient put on the sash? (Self-apply, presented by leader, etc.)**
   - *Why*: Interaction design
   - *Listen for*: Physicality, dignity, ceremony tradition

6. **How much time will the sash be visible on stage?**
   - *Why*: Visual prominence
   - *Listen for*: Long exposure or quick moment, photography opportunity

7. **Will there be close-up shots or detailed photography of the sash?**
   - *Why*: Detail visibility
   - *Listen for*: Need for fine detail, material visibility

---

### Section C: Lighting & Visual Impact (12 mins)

8. **What's the lighting design of the stage during the sash presentation moment?**
   - *Why*: Color accuracy under lights
   - *Listen for*: Warm/cool lights, intensity, spotlights, color temperature

9. **Should the sash be visually bold and attention-grabbing, or subtle and elegant?**
   - *Why*: Visual hierarchy
   - *Listen for*: Prominence level, balance with other elements

10. **What colors will look best under the stage lighting conditions?**
    - *Why*: Color selection
    - *Listen for*: Which colors pop under lights, which get washed out

11. **Should the sash coordinate with, contrast against, or complement the ceremony's color scheme?**
    - *Why*: Design integration
    - *Listen for*: Harmony vs. statement positioning

12. **How will the sash photograph against the stage background?**
    - *Why*: Media/PR dimension
    - *Listen for*: Contrast, visibility, aesthetic appeal

---

### Section D: Design Integration (8 mins)

13. **Does the sash need to match or integrate with other ceremonial elements (podium, backdrop, etc.)?**
    - *Why*: Visual consistency
    - *Listen for*: Design constraints, integration requirements

14. **What's the "wow moment" the ceremony is building toward? (How does the sash fit in?)**
    - *Why*: Dramatic importance
    - *Listen for*: Climactic importance, emotional buildup

---

### Section E: Final Reflection (3 mins)

15. **If the sash could define the spirit of the 30-year celebration in one visual moment, what would that be?**
    - *Why*: Design essence
    - *Listen for*: Defining visual, emotional peak
